Investigate a closed fictional environment through fixed narrative choices and pre-authored synthetic records. This educational static-site exercise is source-visible and is not cheat-resistant.

How to play
  1. Pick a perspective below, then Start. Nothing installs, and you never type at a real system.
  2. Read the current brief and objective, then Choose an Action. Locked actions show the prerequisite you still need.
  3. Reason with the case tools: bookmark records in the Evidence Drawer, watch Asset Health, and filter the synthetic Event Timeline (new events reveal as you progress).
  4. Stuck? Open a Learning Hint. Jot thoughts in Local Case Notes — they are inert text and are never run.
  5. Reach an ending, then read the debrief and score. In Paired Mode you hand the same browser to the defender for a linked response.
